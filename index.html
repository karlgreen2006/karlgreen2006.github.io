Welcome to Turkish with Ozlem

Welcome to Turkish With Ozlem. If you're looking to learn the Turkish language, I can provide you with weekly 1 to 1 lessons.

I am Ozlem Akyol, a native Turkish speaker and a certified language teacher, who lives in the United Kingdom. I have taught Turkish since 2007 to adult learners of all levels, along with GCSE and A-level students. I also teach English and have done so since the year 2000.

I have a clear accent from growing up in Ankara, the capital of Turkey, which will allow you to pick up the language more effectively. I use a communicative and interactive approach to help my students learn Turkish in a fun and effective way.
