About Turkish With Ozlem

Turkish With Ozlem is a website that aims to help people learn the Turkish language. Ozlem is offering one-to-one lessons to help you understand and communicate in Turkish. Whether you are a beginner or have an extensive level of knowledge, Ozlem can help you to further improve your Turkish reading, speaking and listening skills.

Turkish With Ozlem was founded in 2023 by Ozlem, a native Turkish speaker and a certified language teacher. She is a teacher at Birmingham Adult Education Service, and has taught Turkish since 2007 to adult learners of all levels, along with GCSE and A-level students.

We hope you enjoy learning Turkish with us. If you have any suggestions, please feel free to contact us. We would love to hear from you.

Happy learning!
