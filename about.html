About Turkish With Ozlem

Turkish With Ozlem is a website that provides online-based one to one and group lessons to support you with learning the Turkish language. The lessons are designed for beginner level, elementary level, lower intermediate, intermediate and advanced level learners.

Ozlem is a native Turkish speaker and a qualified language teacher with extensive years of studying, resource researching, developing teaching methodologies and experience since 2007. She is a teacher at Birmingham Adult Education Service, and has taught Turkish to adult learners of all levels, along with GCSE and A-level students.

Everyones’ learning needs and reasons differ from each other and before the lessons we will assess these to tailor your learning around your needs.

You can join anytime but commitment is necessary to grasp the language.

Please get in touch to take the first step towards learning Turkish.
